This is extra custom stuff on my server. To install, move the folders in here into your custom folder.

"Medieval" are Custom "Medieval Renaissance" Weapons
"Custom Player Models" is used for a VScript Command to use custom models.

Now restart your game for these to take effect.

===============================================================

Alright, alright, you may be asking why? Well, I would use my fastdl to have players download these assets.
However, the server precache limit cannot handle it all and so I'm spliting it into a manual download from github.
